Hose reel drum

Efficient Fire Hose Storage and Deployment

A hose reel drum is a cylindrical spool designed to store and manage fire hoses, water hoses, or industrial hoses, ensuring quick and easy deployment during emergencies. It is commonly used in firefighting, industrial applications, and irrigation systems for effective fluid delivery.
